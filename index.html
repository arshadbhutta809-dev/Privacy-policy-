Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Bhutta Brand ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Tap Count Liquid Glass mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information Collection and Use

Tap Count Liquid Glass is designed to be a private, local-first utility. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers.

  • Local Data: The application stores your "Tap Count" value locally on your device using Android Jetpack DataStore. This data never leaves your device.
  • Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

2. Permissions

To provide a high-fidelity experience, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Vibration/Haptics: Used to provide tactile feedback when you tap the counter or reset the value.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs that collect your data. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any third-party APIs.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, we are fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. However, since all app data is stored within the private storage of the application on your own device, it is protected by the Android operating system's security sandbox.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
